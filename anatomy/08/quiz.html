    1. tricuspid valve

    2. false

    2. tricuspid and mitral valves
  12. There is one pulmonary vein and one pulmonary artery?
    1. true
    2. false

    2. right ventricle

    1. coronary circulation
    2. systemic circuit
    3. pulmonary circuit
